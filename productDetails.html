This GOXAWEE drill comes with a complete drill bit set that can work on both wood and metal. The masonry drill bit is especially great for drilling holes through concrete. The extra accessories enable the electric drill to be more functional & practical. The tool box is very sturdy and conveniently organizes the tool and accessories.

Notes from the Owner

Drill McDrillman

This is the most versatile drill I’ve ever owned. Thanks to its flexible shaft, it can be used in the places where driver bits are hard to reach. The best part is the drill brush, which can be used in conjunction with the hammer drill to easily remove and clean stains.
